Founded in 2023 by a group of food enthusiasts and world travelers, Mystery Flavor Box was born from a simple idea: make culinary exploration accessible to everyone, regardless of where they live.

We partner with small-batch producers, local artisans, and renowned chefs from around the globe to curate authentic ingredients and recipes that tell the story of different cuisines and cultures.
