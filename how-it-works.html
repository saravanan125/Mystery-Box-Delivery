Frequently Asked Questions

When will my first box arrive?

Orders placed before the 15th of the month will receive the current month's box. Orders after the 15th will receive the following month's box. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days from shipping.

Can I skip a month?

Absolutely! You can skip any month from your dashboard with no penalties. Just make sure to skip before the 1st of the month to avoid being charged.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Set your preferences in your dashboard. We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. We can also accommodate most allergies with advance notice.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime with no fees. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship to the US, Canada, UK, and most EU countries. International shipping is free on all subscription plans.

What if an ingredient arrives damaged?

We guarantee the quality of every ingredient. If anything arrives damaged or unsatisfactory, contact us within 48 hours for a full replacement or refund.

How long do ingredients stay fresh?

All ingredients are packed with care and stay fresh for at least 2-3 months when stored properly. We include storage instructions with every box.

Can I gift a subscription?

Yes! Gift subscriptions are available for 1, 3, 6, or 12 months. You can include a personalized message and choose the start date.
