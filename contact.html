Common Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions

How do I track my order?

Once your box ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your delivery in real-time through your dashboard.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes! You can update your delivery address in your account settings up to 5 days before your box ships.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Our boxes can be safely left at your door. All perishable items are vacuum-sealed and insulated to stay fresh for 24 hours.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@mysteryflavor.com, call us at +1 (555) 123-4567, or use the live chat on our website during business hours.

Do you offer corporate or bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special pricing for corporate gifts and bulk orders. Contact us at corporate@mysteryflavor.com for more information.
